Due Wattana Songpetchmongkol (b. 1996, Bangkok)

is an urban researcher and creative designer who studies the dynamic relationship triad of pedestrians, walking and the city. His interest focuses on walking, street life, urban informality, global binary, the underlying urban fabrics in contemporary cities, and its 'urban drama'. He is interested in how people, goods and information move (around) the city.
