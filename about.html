Due Wattana Songpetchmongkol
published as Wattana Songpetchmongkol

Urban researcher and creative designer studying the dynamic relationship between pedestrians, walking, and the city. His interest focuses on walking, street life, urban informality, global binary, the underlying urban fabrics in contemporary cities, and its 'urban drama'. He is interested in how people, goods and information move (around) the city.
